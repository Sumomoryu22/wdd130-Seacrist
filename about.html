ABOUT ME

CALL ME AMANDA.

I was diagnosed with autism under the DSM-IV, and my journey has been anything but ordinary. Along the way I've celebrated meaningful accomplishments while also facing experiences that challenged my understanding of myself, my future, and the world around me.

Like a phoenix rising after the fire, and like light refracting through a prism, I discovered that difficult experiences don't have to be the end of the story. They can become the beginning of something stronger, wiser, and filled with hope.

NeuroPhoenix was created to reflect that belief. Here I share research, lived experience, practical resources, and encouragement for autistic and ADHD individuals, trauma survivors, families, and allies who are looking for understanding, healing, and brighter days ahead.

"No matter where your journey begins, I hope you'll find a place of acceptance, learning, and hope here."
