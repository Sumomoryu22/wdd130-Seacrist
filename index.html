Our Purpose

WHY WE EXIST.

NeuroPhoenix is to provide a supportive, educational, and empowering resource for autistic and ADHD individuals, as well as those seeking to better understand neurodiversity. We exist to help promote understanding, confidence, and provide a greater sense of belonging for neurodivergent individuals and their communities.

Our Mission

RISING ABOVE CHALLENGES, EMBRACING AUTHENTICITY.

01 - Understand

Diving into complex issues such as ableism, trauma, communication differences, and lived experiences.

02 - Empower

Providing research-based information and coping strategies to help navigate everyday structural challenges.

03 - Belong

Building a community spaces that emphasizes resilience, growth, and the confidence to stop masking.

Who This Is For

THIS SPACE WAS MADE FOR YOU IF YOU ARE...

PRIMARY AUDIENCE

Autistic & ADHD Individuals

Teens and adults navigating Level 1 Autism Spectrum Disorder, ADHD, executive dysfunction, or similar support needs.

Those Newly Discovering Neurodivergence

People seeking deep discussions about masking, burnout, self-advocacy, and processing personal trauma history.

SECONDARY AUDIENCE

Families, Educators & Allies

Parents, friends, and employers wanting to bypass stereotypes and implement truly meaningful support paths.

Those Seeking Deeper Understanding

Mental health practitioners and communities exploring real-world lived intersections of neurodiversity and resilience.
